Central King Buidling

Originally a highschool, the Central King Building started as Newark's Central High School. The Central King Buidling was built in 1911 and features many architectural features that show off the Beaux-Arts architecture style. Throughout the entire building many intracate and detailed features can be seen.

The Central King Building went through a massive renovation in 2017 to retain and restore it historic character and nature but to also turn it into a functional academic hub for NJIT. Many of the restored and perserve features of the Central King Building were kept even after the rennovation. Features like a sweep staircase, arched windows, and detailed moldings are evident throughout the entire structure. Many upgrades were also added to the building. Upgrades like smart classrooms, advanced labs, and collaboration spaces.
